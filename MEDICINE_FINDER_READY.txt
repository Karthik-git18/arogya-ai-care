📝 HOW TO USE:

1. Type a symptom: "fever", "headache", "cough", etc.
2. Click "Find Medicine" button OR Press Enter
3. See results instantly with:
   • Medicine names
   • Dosage
   • When to take
   • Precautions
   • Medical disclaimer
